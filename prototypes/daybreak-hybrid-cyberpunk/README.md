# Daybreak Hybrid - Cyberpunk

A high-contrast cyberpunk variant with neon pink and electric cyan accents.

## Design Philosophy

This variant cranks up the contrast with vivid neon colors inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics. The hot pink and electric cyan create an energetic, futuristic feel that pops against the dark background.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background Primary | Near Black | `#0a0a0b` |
| Background Surface | Dark Gray | `#111113` |
| Accent Primary | Neon Pink | `#ff2d92` |
| Accent Secondary | Electric Cyan | `#00f0ff` |
| Text Primary | Off White | `#fafafa` |

## Visual Characteristics

- **High contrast** — Vivid neons against deep blacks
- **Electric glows** — Stronger glow effects on buttons and accents
- **Complementary pair** — Pink and cyan sit opposite on the color wheel
- **Futuristic vibe** — Evokes sci-fi interfaces and neon cityscapes

## Use Cases

Best suited for:
- Tech products targeting younger demographics
- Gaming or entertainment platforms
- Bold, attention-grabbing landing pages
- Projects wanting maximum visual impact
