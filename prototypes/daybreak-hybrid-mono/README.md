# Daybreak Hybrid - Mono

A minimalist monochrome variant with white and gray accents.

## Design Philosophy

This variant strips away color to focus purely on contrast and form. The white-on-black palette creates maximum legibility while the gray secondary color adds subtle hierarchy without distraction.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background Primary | Near Black | `#0a0a0b` |
| Background Surface | Dark Gray | `#111113` |
| Accent Primary | White | `#ffffff` |
| Accent Secondary | Medium Gray | `#666666` |
| Text Primary | Off White | `#fafafa` |

## Visual Characteristics

- **Maximum contrast** — Pure white accents on deep black
- **Minimal distraction** — No color competition for attention
- **Subtle depth** — Gray secondary provides quiet hierarchy
- **Timeless feel** — Classic black and white never goes out of style

## Use Cases

Best suited for:
- Luxury or premium brands
- Typography-focused designs
- Professional/enterprise applications
- Accessible designs (high contrast ratio)
