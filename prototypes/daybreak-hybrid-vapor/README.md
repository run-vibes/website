# Daybreak Hybrid - Vapor

A dreamy retrowave variant with soft pink and lavender accents.

## Design Philosophy

This variant embraces the vaporwave aesthetic with softer, more pastel-leaning colors. The soft pink and lavender create a nostalgic, dreamy atmosphere that feels retro yet modern.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background Primary | Near Black | `#0a0a0b` |
| Background Surface | Dark Gray | `#111113` |
| Accent Primary | Soft Pink | `#ff71ce` |
| Accent Secondary | Lavender | `#b967ff` |
| Text Primary | Off White | `#fafafa` |

## Visual Characteristics

- **Dreamy glow** — Softer, more diffuse glow effects
- **Pastel neons** — Less harsh than cyberpunk, more approachable
- **Analogous harmony** — Pink and purple sit adjacent on the color wheel
- **Nostalgic vibe** — Evokes 80s/90s retrofuturism

## Use Cases

Best suited for:
- Creative or artistic platforms
- Music and entertainment products
- Brands with retro or nostalgic positioning
- Projects wanting a softer, more approachable tech aesthetic
