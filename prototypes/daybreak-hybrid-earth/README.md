# Daybreak Hybrid - Earth

An organic tech variant with amber and teal accents.

## Design Philosophy

This variant balances technology with nature through warm amber and cool teal. The earth-inspired palette creates a grounded, sustainable feel that's less cold than typical tech aesthetics.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background Primary | Near Black | `#0a0a0b` |
| Background Surface | Dark Gray | `#111113` |
| Accent Primary | Amber | `#f59e0b` |
| Accent Secondary | Teal | `#14b8a6` |
| Text Primary | Off White | `#fafafa` |

## Visual Characteristics

- **Warm-cool balance** — Amber warmth tempered by teal coolness
- **Natural resonance** — Colors evoke earth, sun, and sea
- **Complementary pair** — Amber and teal create visual tension
- **Grounded tech** — Technology that feels connected to nature

## Use Cases

Best suited for:
- Sustainability-focused products
- Fintech and enterprise applications
- Brands emphasizing trust and stability
- Projects wanting warmth without sacrificing professionalism
